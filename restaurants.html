Our history

BurgerHub was born out of pure love for burgers. We saw how much our friends and families liked it,so one day we just decided to start sharing it with the world. We started with a small burger bar. Who know'ss if it's gonna work out, right? Then BurgerHub got popular so we opend more restaurants slowly.And today we are proud to have 4 different locations in Belgium and we are thinking of international expansion in The Netherlands and in Germany.

Our philosophy

We trully believ that in order to make good food, you MUST use good ingredients. For us it's a must! We collaborate with local producers, to ensure our products are always fresh. Which also helps us to reduce our environmental footprint and contribute to local economy.
